Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Zero PII Guarantee

AnonID is built on a strict Zero-PII (Personally Identifiable Information) architecture. We do not collect, store, or process names, emails, physical addresses, or any traditional identifying information from your end-users.

2. IP Addresses

We do not collect or store raw IP addresses. IP addresses are subjected to a one-way cryptographic hash with a namespace salt immediately upon arrival. The raw IP is discarded and never written to disk. The resulting hash is used exclusively for localized network clustering to prevent fraud.

3. Browser Fingerprinting

We utilize anonymous browser signals (such as screen resolution, hardware concurrency, and canvas rendering traits) to generate a deterministic visitor hash. This hash cannot be reverse-engineered to identify a human being and is strictly scoped to the requesting domain's namespace.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at support@anonid.pro.
